CREATOR

LUIS R. GUTIERREZ

Luis Gutierrez zealous passion for the youth fostered an evocative vision that
turned into phenomenal reality.

Even before his appointment to the San Jose Del Monte City Youth and Sports
Development Office (SJDM-CYSDO), Gutierrez has always been a tireless
advocator of activities for the betterment of the youth. One of his voluntary
works, which started since 2006, is to provide educational tools and materials
to children such as notebooks, crayons, pens, pencils, and so on. With his strong
determination to help and his partnership and coordination with exceptionally
supportive organizations and individuals, he secures the success of each
program he creates.

A true leader is born to make a difference; to lead and to inspire; to influence
and to deliver outstanding results; and to produce creations that secures the
amelioration of the people. Gutierrez leadership and passion led him to the
creation of QUICK 2 GAME.
