HISTORY

QUICK 2 GAME is a word game played by two to four players by forming words
using lettered tiles strategically placed on a flat surface in crossword fashion.
The game challenges intelligence and fast-thinking, which players actively
play under pressure.

QUICK 2 GAME:

·Supports the educational need of the Filipinos, particularly the youth, by
building vocabulary and word proficiencies, enhancing language abilities,
establishing social skills and developing strategical and critical thinking while
having fun.
·Prevents the youth from internet addiction.
·Gathers word game enthusiasts in which they encourage one another and to
influence the youth to expose themselves into a more interactive activity, which
facilitates learning and skills development.
